## Additional Visual Signals

In addition to text and transcript signals, evaluate the provided keyframes for:
- **Lighting uniformity**: AI-generated scenes tend toward uniform lighting
- **Background complexity**: Real environments have clutter; AI backgrounds are clean
- **Text overlays**: Style, placement, and font choices
- **Facial presence**: Real faces vs. AI-generated faces (uncanny valley markers)
- **Camera motion patterns**: Handheld vs. synthetic smooth motion
