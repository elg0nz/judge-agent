## Additional Transcript Signals

In addition to the text signals, evaluate:
- **Disfluency markers**: Presence/absence of "um", "uh", false starts, self-corrections
- **Pacing patterns**: Consistent cadence suggests AI voiceover; natural speech varies
- **Topic tangents**: Human speakers digress and recover; AI stays relentlessly on-topic
- **Filler words and timing gaps** (from SRT/VTT timing data if available)
