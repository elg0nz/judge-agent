# Assignment — Feltsense Take-Home

## 🧪 Your Challenge: Build a Judge Agent

Your task is to build a small "judge agent" that can evaluate both text and video content and produce:

- A prediction of whether the content is AI-generated or human-generated
- A virality score (likelihood the content would perform well socially)
- A short distribution analysis (which audiences or communities it might resonate with and why)
- A concise explanation of why the agent produced each output

We intentionally keep this specification open. We're looking for your reasoning, the assumptions you choose, and the design decisions you make along the way.

## ⏱ Time & Deadline

- Please spend no more than ~6 hours of actual work
- You have 48 hours from the time this email was sent to submit your solution

## 📦 What to Submit

Please reply with:

- A public GitHub repository containing your code
- A README that includes:
  - What you built (2–4 sentences)
  - How to run it (setup + commands)
  - Any assumptions you made
  - What you would improve with more time
- (Optional) A short Loom (2–4 min) walking through your approach

We are evaluating clarity, reasoning, and how you approach ambiguity — not polish or perfection.

## ❓ Clarifications

If anything is unclear, feel free to send one clarifying email with your question(s). We'll reply as quickly as possible.

## 📅 What Happens Next
